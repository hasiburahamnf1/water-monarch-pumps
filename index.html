Key pump failures • Process issues: Such failures generally occur due to improperly selected pump meant for specific operating conditions, industrial applications or process. Right pump should be chosen for the specific application.

• Mechanical seal leakage: Leakages mostly tend to occur at interface of two seal faces. Besides this, leakages might even occur in secondary sealing area. Mechanical seal leakage could occur for various reasons. It includes seal ring misalignment, thermal distortion and dry running, etc.

• Coupling related issues: There are several reasons for which coupling failures might occur. It includes improper lubrication, torsional vibration, uncommon environmental causes, overload, misalignment, etc.

• Impeller wear & tear: It might occur due to several reasons of which the common one is erosion. Even improperly sized pump might cause pressure imbalance.

• Bearing issues: Improper lubrication is rather cited to be the main reason for bearings to stop functioning. This is due to fatigue, overload, etc.
